FAQs

How do I track my order?

After placing your order, you can track its status in the "My Orders" section of your account. Click on the specific order, and you'll find real-time tracking updates along with the estimated delivery date.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) for select locations.

How can I return or exchange a product?

To return or exchange a product, go to the "My Orders" section, select the item, and click on the "Return/Exchange" option. Follow the on-screen instructions and choose the reason for return or exchange. Ensure the product is in its original condition.

Is my personal and payment information secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption technology and secure payment gateways to ensure your personal and financial information is safe. Our platform complies with data protection regulations.

What should I do if my product is damaged or defective?

If you receive a damaged or defective product, report the issue within 7 days of delivery through the "Help Center" or "My Orders" section. We’ll arrange a replacement or refund as per our policy.
